HOW IT WORKS

Six-stage evaluation pipeline.

Multilingual Intent Detection

Classifies borrower intent across English, Hindi, Bengali, and Hinglish. Fine-tuned TF-IDF classifier with ALREADY_PAID keyword override. Supports 6 intent classes including DISPUTE, HIGH_RISK, and VAGUE.

Risk & Promise Scoring

XGBoost risk scorer (0–1) with feature importance analysis. Promise parser extracts payment timelines from 95+ multilingual temporal patterns across all four supported languages.

RBI Compliance Engine

25 RBI-grounded rules across threats, harassment, coercion, abusive language, and false claims. 233 detection patterns, zero false positives on the test set.
